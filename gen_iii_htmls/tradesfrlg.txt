<p><font size="3"><b><u>In-Game Trades</u></b></font></p>
<p>Like in Pokemon Red and Blue, there are a few times when you'll be asked to trade Pokemon with a non-player character. These trades don't differ much from the original games, and outside of Pokemon XD are still the only way to obtain certain Pokemon. There are, however, a few version exclusive trades, mostly capitalizing on the fact that certain Pokemon are only available in certain versions. Similar to Ruby and Sapphire, these Pokemon have a specific contest stat enhanced, but only in games where contests exist. Certain Pokemon also carry special items.</p>
