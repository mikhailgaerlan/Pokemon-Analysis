<b>Location</b>: Celadon City</p>

<p><b>Method</b>:<br />
The back door of the Celadon Mansion leads to a secret entrance with several flights of stairs. At the very top there's a room where an Eevee awaits, an extremely flexible Normal-type Pokemon. What makes Eevee so special is that it can evolve into five different Pokemon: Flareon, the Fire-type, Vaporeon, the Water-type, Jolteon, the Electric-type, Espeon, the Psychic-type, and Umbreon, the Dark-type.
